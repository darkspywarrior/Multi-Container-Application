❓ 13. Expected Review Panel Questions

Q1: Why Hyperledger Fabric over append-only logs or Ethereum?
A: Permissioned, privacy, endorsement policies, no gas costs.

Q2: Why store only hashes on-chain instead of full files?
A: Scalability — files in MinIO, hashes for integrity proof.

Q3: What attacks does this specifically stop?
A: Undetected tampering, forged audit logs, admin abuse.

Q4: Why not just use a secure database with audit logs?
A: Centralized logs can be altered by admin — Fabric prevents.

Q5: What is your trust model?
A: 2-of-2 endorsement between independent organizations.
